What data types do you work with?

scRNA-seq, bulk RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, CITE-seq, proteomics (mass-spec), metabolomics, spatial transcriptomics, and any combination of these for multi-omics integration.

Do I need a long-term contract?

No. Every engagement is a one-off, fixed-price project. No retainers, no minimums, no ongoing commitment.

How do you handle confidentiality?

All data stays in secure, access-controlled environments. I sign NDAs and data processing agreements as standard practice. No client data is used for content or shared publicly.

I'm used to university contracts with hourly billing. How does fixed-price work?

After a free scoping call, I quote a single price for the full deliverable. You pay per project, not per hour — so you know the total cost upfront, and I'm incentivized to deliver efficiently.
