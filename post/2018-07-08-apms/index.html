CompPASS

CompPASS was first proposed to elucidate human deubiquitinating enzyme interaction network by Sowa et al. The output includes Z-score, S-score, D-score and WD-score. In its implementation in BioPlex 1.0 and BioPlex 2.0, a naive Bayes classifier that learns to distinguish true interacting proteins from non-specific background and false positive identifications was included in the compPASS pipline.
