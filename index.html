About

I am currently wrapping up thesis of the PhD program in Dr. Mohan Babu's group, University of Regina. In light of the deluge of omics data in life sciences, my research interests focus on computational approaches in systems biology including developing gene expression database, Bioconductor R package. My recent publication revealed rewiring the mitochondrial interactome during neurogensis using both biochemical fractionation proteomics and single cell RNA-seq.
