About

Learn more about me

Systems Biologist & Web Developer

I am currently working as a PDF in Dr. Marjorie Brand's lab, OHRI, Ottawa, Ontario. In light of the deluge of omics data in life sciences, my research interests focus on computational approaches in systems biology including developing gene expression database, SMAD (Bioconductor R package).

My publications include mitochondrial interactome and single cell RNA-seq during neurogensis, mitochondrial interactome from AP-MS to understand neurodegeneration, elucidating global landscape of cell envelope protein complexes in Escherichia coli and Renal Gene Expression Database (RGED) to explore expression profiles in kidney diseases.

Have been working in bioinformatics field for more than 10 years, I have accumulated hands-on experiences in

  • Bulk and single cell RNA-seq
  • ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq and CUT&Tag
  • HiC, CaptureC, HiCAR
  • WES and WGS
  • Proteomics in AP-MS and Co-Fraction
  • Statistical modelling and machine learning
  • UNIX Shell Perl scripting
  • Web application development
  • High performance computing
  • Cloud computing
